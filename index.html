About Apeiron

Apeiron Management has developed an innovative operating model, utilising highly skilled global advisers to offer services at a much lower cost without compromising quality and safety. Key outcomes from this model are increasing productivity and efficiency to complex analytical problems through our expertise and knowledge gained from years of advising.

Our goal is to develop a trusting relationship with clients to help achieve their business goals. We work with clients to maximise shareholder returns by identifying business drivers which most influence value, reduce costs and increase digitalisation using our disruptive operating model.

We evaluate petroleum systems, play fairways, drillable prospects, geological risking and exploration economics using our deep domain expertise in geophysics and geology interpretation. Our team specializes in seismic attribute analysis, rock physics, sedimentology and structural controls.

We offer our technical and commercial multidisciplinary approach to support a government agency to prepare asset data sets, data management, marketing and promoting to investors, planning data room meetings and facilitating agreements with investors.

Our specialists have managed exploration, multidisciplinary teams with international exploration experience. Our associates have special expertise in structural geology, sedimentology and geochemistry.

We evaluate blocks in licensing rounds, document resource opportunities (DRO) on offer in marginal field bid rounds and opportunities originating from our activities in farm-in, M&A and divestments.

Our work scope includes regional reviews, play and prospect evaluations, risking and applications expected monetary value (EMV) results from economic modeling.
